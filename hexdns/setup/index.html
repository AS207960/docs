    Adding your zone to HexDNS¶

    To add your zone to HexDNS login to the control panel here, and follow the zone creation wizard. You won't be able to create a zone if someone else already controls a more generic zone.

    Once the zone is created set the nameservers on the parent to:

    • ns1.as207960.net
    • ns2.as207960.net

    You may also want to configure DNSSEC
